ABOUT

SecCom 2023

30 Nov & 1 Dec, 2023

This programme will be hosted by the Department of Cyber Science & Technology in association with Brainware Cyber Club (BCC) and Institution Innovation Council (IIC), Brainware University.

Celebrating Computer Security Day with a focus on cybersecurity is a great initiative to raise awareness and promote best practices in the field. We provide your track with unparalleled exposure by assemblingb professionals from the IT and cybersecurity industries, developers, leaders, students, and entrepreneurs under one roof.

Objectives

Engaging Speakers

Renowned professionals are actively identifying and addressing current trends in their fields to stay updated and mitigate potential threats. They aim to establish a robust framework for client safety and security, demonstrating a commitment to excellence and providing unparalleled services that meet the ever-changing demands of their industries. Their vigilant efforts ensure the safety and security of their clients, demonstrating their commitment to staying ahead of the curve in their respective fields.

Learn More...
Capture The Flag Competetion

The Capture The Flag (CTF) competition is a dynamic event that enhances cybersecurity skills through real-world scenarios and challenges. It fosters problem-solving, critical thinking, and collaborative teamwork. The gamified approach showcases technical prowess and cultivates a culture of continuous learning. The interactive nature elevates individual capabilities and contributes to collective advancement of cybersecurity expertise, making it a crucial arena for professional growth and community development in digital security.

Learn More...
Panel Discussions

Experts can address current issues and potential solutions in panel discussions on cybersecurity, which encourage audience participation and inquiry. These seminars encourage audience engagement by bringing together seasoned professionals to exchange a range of viewpoints and tactics. Panel discussions enable participants to actively participate in the conversation and foster a deeper awareness of cybersecurity challenges by fostering an interactive environment. This cooperative strategy improves knowledge exchange and influences the current discourse on cybersecurity issues and solutions.

Learn More...
Community Outreach

Cybersecurity awareness sessions are essential for educating and raising awareness within local communities. They equip individuals with knowledge about cyber threats and best practices, fostering a safer environment. By sharing practical information and real-world examples, these sessions empower community members to recognize and mitigate cyber risks. The interactive nature of these sessions encourages open discussions, tailoring information to specific community needs. Overall, they build resilience and cultivate a culture of vigilance and responsibility.

Learn More...
Networking Opportunities

The exchange of knowledge is crucial for professionals and enthusiasts to build connections and relationships, making networking vital for professional growth. These spaces serve as platforms where individuals share insights, experiences, and industry trends. Through networking, professionals expand their knowledge and establish connections that can lead to collaborative opportunities and mentorship. Whether physical or virtual, these spaces create dynamic forums where individuals can leverage each other's strengths, fostering a sense of community and continuous learning for sustained success in their respective fields.

Learn More...
Exhibition Area

A dedicated cybersecurity exhibition area offers companies and organizations a platform to showcase their products and services. Attendees can explore the latest advancements and solutions in the cybersecurity landscape, gain insights into emerging trends, and engage with cybersecurity companies. This interactive environment facilitates hands-on learning, encourages meaningful discussions, and empowers attendees to make informed decisions about integrating robust cybersecurity measures. It serves as a hub for knowledge exchange, networking, and promoting collaborative partnerships.
