ABOUT

SecCom 2023

30 Nov & 1 December, 2023

This programme will be hosted by the Department of Cyber Science & Technology in association with Brainware Cyber Club and Institution Innovation Council (IIC), Brainware University.

Celebrating Computer Security Day with a focus on cybersecurity is a great initiative to raise awareness and promote best practices in the field. We provide your track with unparalleled exposure by assembling professionals from the IT and cybersecurity industries, developers, leaders, students, and entrepreneurs under one roof.
